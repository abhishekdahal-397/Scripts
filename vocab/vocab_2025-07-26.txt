📖 Word: formatted

🔢 Meaning 1
🔤 Part of Speech: verb
📝 Definition: To create or edit the layout of a document.

🔢 Meaning 2
🔤 Part of Speech: verb
📝 Definition: Change a document so it will fit onto a different type of page.

🔢 Meaning 3
🔤 Part of Speech: verb
📝 Definition: To prepare a mass storage medium for initial use, erasing any existing data in the process.
📌 Example 1: I lost weeks of work when I inadvertently formatted my hard drive.
